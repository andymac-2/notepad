# Checklists

Follow these checklists to prevent mistakes. These are useful in Hard mode playthroughs. Consider adapting them to your needs.

## Rocket Construction

- Staging
- Delta V
- Fins/Aero
- Crew/Pods
- RCS/Reaction wheels
- Power
- Signal
- Heat shields
- Parachutes
- Science
- Landing
- Flight plan and Contracts
- Custom actions

## Construction of Mining vessel

- Convert-O-tron
- Drill
- Power: Solar panels and/or Fuel cells
- Ore container
- Heat dissipation

## Pre-flight

- Transfer Window
- Crew
- Go to launchpad
- Staging
- Flight plan
- Go over abort options
- SAS
- Throttle
- Stage

## Pre warp

- Solar panels extended

## Atmospheric Pre-entry

- Go over bail out scenario
- Heat shields
- Parachutes
- Landing site (water, day, flat, etc)
- Periapsis

## Atmospheric Entry

- Burn off any remaining fuel to slow down
- Equip personal parachutes
- Bail out if needed
- Landing speed under 6m/s

## Rover requirements

- Wheels
- Power
    - Batteries and solar panels
    - Fuel cells and fuel
- Control
    - Command seat
    - Probe core/Antenna
- Reaction wheel
